# Patent Non-Assertion Policy

**Version 1.0 — Effective April 2026**

Orkia ("Licensor") makes the following unilateral patent
non-assertion commitment in respect of this repository.

## 1. Definitions

- **"Software"** means the source code, Rust crates, CLI
  binaries, scripts, configuration, examples, and documentation
  distributed in this repository under the Elastic License 2.0
  (see [LICENSE](./LICENSE)), together with any
  vendored or forked components attributed in
  [NOTICE](./NOTICE) and
  [THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES.md](./THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES.md).
- **"Patent Claim"** means any claim of a patent now owned,
  controlled, or licensable by Licensor anywhere in the world.
- **"User"** means any person or entity exercising rights in
  the Software under the applicable licence.

## 2. Non-Assertion Commitment

Licensor covenants not to assert any Patent Claim against any
User using the Software, provided that:

(a) the Patent Claim reads on functionality of the Software as
    distributed by Licensor (i.e. the patent is necessarily
    infringed by the Software in its distributed form);
(b) the User's use complies with the
    [LICENSE](./LICENSE) (Elastic License 2.0).

## 3. Termination for Patent Aggression

The commitment in Section 2 terminates automatically and
immediately, with respect to the aggressor and any entity
under common control with the aggressor, if that person
initiates patent litigation (including cross-claims and
counter-claims) against Licensor, against the Software, or
against any contributor alleging that the Software or a
contribution to the Software infringes a patent.

This mirrors the termination clause in Apache License 2.0 § 3.

This policy covers patents owned or controlled by Licensor
only.

The Software incorporates vendored and forked third-party

full list. Patents covering those third-party components, and

CLI binaries that the User installs separately), remain
governed by their respective licences. This policy does not
purport to grant any licence under those third-party patents.

## 5. No Waiver of Trademark Rights

This policy covers patents only. It does not licence any
trademark. See [TRADEMARK.md](./TRADEMARK.md) for trademark
policy.

## 6. Future Assignment

If Licensor transfers ownership of this repository to a
successor legal entity formed to hold the Orkia project -
including any company, corporation, limited liability company,
or similar entity formed by the founders of Orkia - the
successor is bound by this policy on the same terms as a
condition of the transfer. Users of the Software acquire no
lesser rights as a result of such assignment.

## 7. Contributions

Contributions to this repository are made under the terms of
the applicable licence (see [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md)
and [LICENSE](./LICENSE)). By submitting a contribution,
contributors also make the same non-assertion commitment
described in Section 2 with respect to any patents they own or
control at the time of the contribution, to the extent the
patent reads on the contribution.
